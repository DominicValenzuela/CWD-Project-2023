Cheesecakemore_vert
INGREDIENTS:close

1. Butter, for coating the pan

2. 2 pounds full-fat cream cheese.

3. 1 cup granulated sugar.

4. 1 tablespoon cornstarch, or 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (optional).

5. 1/8 teaspoon salt.

6. 1/2 cup sour cream.

7. 2 teaspoons freshly squeezed lemon juice (optional).

8. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.

9. 3 large eggs.

10. 1 large egg yolk.


FOR THE CRUST:

1. 12 whole graham cracker rectangles (6 ounces).

2. 5 tablespoons unsalted butter

Banana Bread with Chocolate Chipsmore_vert
INGREDIENTS:close

1. 1 3/4 cups (8oz | 235g) all-purpose flour.

2. 3/4 cup (5oz | 150 g) granulated white sugar.

3. 1/4 cup (2oz | 50g) brown sugar.

4. 1 teaspoon baking soda.

5. 1/2 teaspoon salt.

6. 1 teaspoon cinnamon.

7. 3 medium ripe bananas (about 1 pound or 500g).

8. 2 large eggs lightly beaten.

9.1/2 cup (4oz | 115 grams) unsalted butter melted and cooled (or cooking oil).

10. 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

11. 1 cup (6 oz | 175 g) chocolate chips (or roughly chopped nuts of your choice)
